In a Nutshell

Hey there! My name is Kaleb Emery, and I am a prospective software developer enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy.

Before this journey, I completed my Bachelor of Science at Ball State University. My passion for marketing has only grown after my studies came to an end in 2018. Since then, I have learned a lot about myself, specifically where my heart is in regards to what I want out of my career.

I believe software development is my perfect crossroad between creative and analytical thinking. I am eagerly looking forward to the obstacles I will face in my career as a developer, and expect it to be just as rewarding as it is challenging.

In my freetime I love to bike, try new recipes and cuisine, and I am always on the lookout for new live music venues and festivals.
